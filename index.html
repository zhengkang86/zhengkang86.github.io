About Me

I am a senior research scientist at PAII Inc. I received my PhD in Computer Science and Engineering from University of South Carolina in 2019. Before that, I received my bachelor degree in Electrical Engineering from Harbin Institute of Technology in 2012.

My research interests are mainly computer vision, deep learning, and medical image analysis.
